# Roblox Game Building — Strategy & Mission

## Mission

Build one breakout Roblox game in a proven genre, paired with a YouTube channel documenting the build and gameplay. The game monetizes through Robux; the channel monetizes through AdSense and sponsors. Same audience, two revenue streams, one flywheel.

---

## The Ecosystem Logic

```
YouTube channel (build + gameplay content)
    → Audience growth
        → Players enter the game
            → Game passes + developer products = Robux
                → DevEx cash out = real USD
        → AdSense + sponsor revenue from the channel
```

This mirrors how every other department works: content drives audience, audience drives revenue.

---

## What We're Building

**Genre:** Simulator or Tycoon (highest monetization potential, proven demand)

**Why these genres:**
- Simulator and tycoon games dominate Roblox revenue charts
- Natural loop: collect → upgrade → show off → pay to accelerate
- Easy to understand, easy to market, easy to expand with updates
- Developer products (repeatable purchases) are the core monetization mechanic

**What we're NOT building (yet):**
- Maps for other developers — too dependent on B2B sales
- UGC avatar items — gated behind Roblox approval, highly competitive
- Complex RPGs or AAA-scope games — need large teams to compete

---

## How the Money Works

**Currency:** Robux (players buy with real money, ~100 Robux = ~$1 retail)

**Earn Robux via:**
| Method | Type | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Developer Products | Repeatable purchase | Coins, boosts, revives — core loop |
| Game Passes | One-time purchase | VIP, double XP, exclusive areas |
| Private Servers | Subscription | Players pay to host their own server |
| Premium Payouts | Passive | Roblox pays based on Premium subscriber time in-game |

**Cash out via DevEx (Developer Exchange):**
- Minimum 30,000 earned Robux to be eligible
- Rate: ~100,000 Robux ≈ $350 (Roblox takes ~65%)
- Paid out in USD to your account

## Revenue Benchmarks

| Tier | Annual Robux Revenue |
|------|---------------------|
| Top games (Adopt Me, Bloxburg) | $10M–$50M+/year |
| Mid-tier successful game | $50K–$500K/year |
| Long tail | Near zero |

The market is hit-driven. One breakout > ten mediocre games.

## Build Sequence

1. Choose a specific simulator or tycoon concept (narrow niche > broad)
2. Build a playable MVP — core loop only, nothing extra
3. Launch YouTube channel alongside the game (build-in-public format)
4. Monetize game with one game pass and one developer product first
5. Grow both simultaneously — content drives players, players validate the game
6. Expand based on what players actually ask for
7. Scale the monetization as audience grows
