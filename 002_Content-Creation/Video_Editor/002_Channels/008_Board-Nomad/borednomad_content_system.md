# BORED NOMAD — CHANNEL CONTENT SYSTEM

## CORE IDENTITY

- **Brand:** Bored Nomad
- **Tagline:** Travel guides, food adventures, and the digital nomad life.
- **Tone:** Practical, warm, slightly playful — the well-traveled friend who actually gives you the useful info

## THUMBNAIL SYSTEM

### Style
- Clean, bright backgrounds — not dark/moody like NeonParcel or Reimagined Realms
- One strong visual subject: landmark, food dish, animal, or map
- Bold white text with dark outline/shadow for readability
- Warm tones dominant — teal, orange, sand — matches brand palette
- Often includes a country flag emoji or map element
- High contrast, information-first — viewer should know instantly where and what

### Layout
- Text: Top-left or full-width across top
- Subject: Center or right — clear and unambiguous
- Ranking/list thumbnails: numbered overlay or comparison split

### Rules
**DO:**
- Use vibrant, travel-postcard color grading
- Show the destination or food clearly — no mystery shots
- Include a clear hook word: "BEST," "GUIDE," "RANKED," "HOW TO"
- Match the teal/orange palette when possible

**DON'T:**
- No moody dark aesthetics — this channel is warm and inviting
- No overly cluttered text — one hook, one subject
- Don't be vague — Bored Nomad viewers want to know exactly what they're getting
- No stock-photo generic shots — specificity wins

## TITLE SYSTEM

### Format
- Practical and direct: "[City/Country] [Topic] Guide"
- Or: "Best [Food/Places] in [City] — Ranked"
- Or: "[Specific Practical How-To] in [Destination]"
- Often includes emoji for destination flag or food category (1–2 max)

### Examples
- "Japan Suica Card Guide — Everything You Need to Know 🇯🇵"
- "Istanbul Street Food Ranked 🇹🇷 (Honest Review)"
- "Wild Animals in Bali You'll Actually See 🦎"
- "Best eSIM for Traveling Asia — Airalo Review"
- "Digital Nomad Health Insurance: Is Safetywing Worth It?"

### Title Patterns That Work
- `[Country/City] [Specific Topic] Guide`
- `[Number] Best [Food/Places] in [City] — Ranked`
- `[Practical Tool/Service] for [Travel Context] — Is It Worth It?`
- `Wild Animals / Wildlife in [Destination]`

---

## HOOK SYSTEM

### First 3 Seconds (Shorts)
- Show the destination or food instantly — no preamble
- Or: bold text overlay with the core question/claim: "This card saves you $30 every trip in Japan"

### First 10 Seconds (Long-form)
- State the practical value upfront: "If you're going to [destination], here's what you actually need to know"
- Show 2–3 quick visuals of what's coming
- Avoid travel-vlog meandering — get to the useful part fast
