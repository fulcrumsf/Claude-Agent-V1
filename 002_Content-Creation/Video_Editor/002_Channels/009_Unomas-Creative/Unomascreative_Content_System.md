# UNO MAS CREATIVE — CHANNEL CONTENT SYSTEM

## CORE IDENTITY

- **Brand:** Uno Mas Creative
- **Tagline:** Design tutorials for creators at every level.
- **Tone:** Clear, professional, approachable — working creative teaching other working creatives
- **Audience:** Designers, marketers, small business owners, freelancers learning tools, ages 22–50
- **Concept:** Tutorial and education channel tied to the Uno Mas Creative LLC brand. Covers Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, graphic design fundamentals, and marketing workflows. Lower-frequency publishing; functions as brand credibility and top-of-funnel for the broader Uno Mas ecosystem.

---

## COLOR SYSTEM

*Palette should align with Uno Mas Creative LLC brand identity.*

Recommended:
- **Deep Navy:** `#1A2744` (professional, trustworthy)
- **Warm Orange:** `#E8631A` (creative energy, action)
- **Clean White:** `#FFFFFF` (negative space, clarity)
- **Light Gray:** `#F2F2F2` (backgrounds, UI overlay)

### Palette Vibe
Agency-adjacent. Clean but not cold. The visual language of a design studio that takes craft seriously but doesn't take itself too seriously.

---

## CONTENT PILLARS

1. **Photoshop tutorials** — practical, task-based (resizing, masking, color, compositing)
2. **Adobe Illustrator tutorials** — vector basics, logo construction, type, illustration
3. **Graphic design fundamentals** — composition, typography, color theory for practitioners
4. **Marketing design** — social media graphics, ad creative, brand kit creation
5. **Tool walkthroughs** — reviews and workflows for design software used in the LLC's work

### Priority Note
This is a lower-priority channel. Publish when useful for the brand; don't force volume. Quality over cadence.

---

## THUMBNAIL SYSTEM

### Style
- Clean, professional — matches agency aesthetic
- Software UI screenshots paired with the finished output
- Bold sans-serif text: the skill taught, clearly stated
- Tool logos visible (Ps, Ai)

### Rules
**DO:**
- "How to" format dominant
- Show before/after or finished result
- Tool clearly identified

**DON'T:**
- No clickbait
- Keep it clean — this reflects the LLC brand

---

## TITLE SYSTEM

### Format
- "How to [Task] in [Photoshop / Illustrator]"
- "[Tool] Tutorial: [Skill] for [Use Case]"
- "[Marketing Task] — Design Tutorial"

### Examples
- "How to Create a Brand Kit in Adobe Illustrator"
- "Photoshop Tutorial: Removing Backgrounds (Non-Destructive)"
- "How to Design Instagram Ad Creative in Photoshop"
- "Adobe Illustrator for Beginners — Logo Design Walkthrough"
