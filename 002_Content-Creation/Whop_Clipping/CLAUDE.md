## What This Is

Long-form video → Shorts pipeline for client brands via Whop. Tony posts on theme pages across Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok. Income is earned per-view.

**Phase:** Planned — not yet building production systems. Focus on understanding the model and designing the clipping workflow first.

## Core Model

1. Long-form videos (from client brands or Tony's own content) are broken into engaging short clips
2. Clips are posted to theme pages on IG/YouTube/TikTok
3. Per-view income is earned through Whop's platform
4. Volume and quality both matter — more high-retention clips = more revenue
