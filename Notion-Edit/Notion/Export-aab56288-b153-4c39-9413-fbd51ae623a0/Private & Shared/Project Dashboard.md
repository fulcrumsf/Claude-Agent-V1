## \#bmode [Brainstorm Prompt]

> [!important] Go into Brainstorm Mode. Short bullet points only, no code, no building examples. Just lists and ideas.
> 
> ### **✅**
> 
> ### **Brainstorm Mode Prompt for ChatGPT**
> 
> > From this point forward, enter “Brainstorm Mode.”
> 
> > In Brainstorm Mode:
> 
> - Respond with **quick, concise replies** (ideally no more than **2–3 sentences**).
> 
> - Focus on **idea generation, structure, and flow** — **not detailed explanations**.
> 
> - Use **simple, human language**, like you’re explaining to a **5th grader**.
> 
> - Include **bullet points** or structured lists for clarity.
> 
> - Use **analogies** or **real-life comparisons** when explaining something complex.
> 
> - **Do not show code**, technical breakdowns, or build steps unless explicitly asked.
> 
> - **Do not offer to elaborate** unless I say something like: “expand,” “go deeper,” or “show example.”
> 
> > This is a rapid idea development space — we’re brainstorming, not building.

### High Stakes Daily Tittok Warming
### **📆**
### **Day 1–3: Account Setup & Engagement Only**
- Create profile (pic, bio, username).
- Watch 20–30 mins of content in your niche daily.
- Like/comment on **5–10 videos per day** (don’t spam).
- **Do NOT post yet.**
---
### **📆**
### **Day 4–7: Light Activity**
- Keep engaging 15–20 mins/day.
- Start following **5–10 creators** in your niche.
- Soft activity → maybe post **1 video total** in this period.
---
### **📆**
### **Week 2: Start Posting Consistently**
- Post **1 video every 1–2 days** (3–4 total in week).
- Keep engagement natural (likes, comments, follows).
- Use TikTok’s **native features** (sounds, text, captions).
---
### **📆**
### **Week 3–4: Ramp Up**
- Move to **1 video per day** if possible.
- Keep retention high: short, punchy clips.
- Continue daily engagement but avoid mass activity.
---
### **⚠️**
### **Key Shadowban Avoidance Tips**
- Don’t post more than **3 videos per day** early on.
- Don’t mass follow/unfollow (stay under ~20/day first weeks).
- Avoid reposting watermarked or copyrighted clips.
- Keep niche consistent — jumping niches confuses the algo.
---
👉 After about **30 days**, your account is considered “warmed,” and you can post aggressively (2–3/day) if you want.
