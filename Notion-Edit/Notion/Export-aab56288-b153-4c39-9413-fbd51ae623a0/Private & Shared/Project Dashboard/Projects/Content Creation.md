### Room Portal Master Table Legend
|Column|Meaning|Purpose / How to Read|
|---|---|---|
|**Theme**|The ambience or soundscape concept being evaluated (e.g., “Tent Camping Rain,” “Ghibli Café”).|The specific video niche idea.|
|**DR (Google Trends Avg)**|**Demand Ratio** – Average Google Trends score (0–100) over the past 12 months (worldwide).|Measures _how many people are searching_ for this topic. Higher = stronger demand.|
|**# Long-form in Top 20**|Number of long videos (≥1 hour) in the top 20 YouTube search results for this niche.|Shows how much “evergreen” content dominates the results. High number = saturated niche.|
|**# New in 90d**|Number of videos in the top 20 that were uploaded within the last 90 days.|Measures _how active_ the niche is right now. High = lots of competition uploading recently.|
|**CI**|**Competition Index** – Calculated as _(# Long-form in Top 20) + (# New in 90d)_.|Combines long-term and recent competition. Higher = more crowded niche.|
|**Top Incumbent (Title + Views)**|The most-viewed or most dominant video in that niche.|Helps you benchmark performance potential.|
|**GS (DR ÷ [CI+1])**|**Gap Score** – A quick metric to balance demand and competition.|Higher = better opportunity (High DR + Low CI). **4.0+ = Excellent**, **1.0–3.9 = Viable**, **<1.0 = Saturated/Low Interest**.|
|**Verdict**|The qualitative summary: _High_, _Medium_, _Low_, etc.|Combines all metrics to quickly guide which ideas are worth prioritizing.|
