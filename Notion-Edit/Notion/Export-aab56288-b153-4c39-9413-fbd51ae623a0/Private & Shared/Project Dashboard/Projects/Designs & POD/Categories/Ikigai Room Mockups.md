## **LIGHTING TYPES**
|**Lighting Type**|**Description**|**Mood Result**|
|---|---|---|
|**Soft Natural Daylight**|Indirect window light, diffused|realistic, bright but gentle|
|**Golden-Hour**|Warm low-angle sunlight|cinematic, cozy, moody highlights|
|**Overcast Light**|Even gray daylight, low contrast|calm, neutral, editorial|
|**Moody Warm Ambient**|Artificial warm light, shadow play|cozy, intimate, design-forward|
|**Cool Diffused Light**|Clean daylight with cool temperature|minimal, Scandinavian tone|
|**Evening Lamp Light**|Dim, localized lamps and sconces|nostalgic, cozy vignette|
|**Backlit / Rim Light**|Glow from behind subject|dramatic, cinematic depth|
|**Directional Spot Light**|Controlled light emphasizing subject|high-contrast, editorial look|
|**Natural Mixed Light**|Blend of daylight and soft lamp|realistic lived-in tone|

|---|---|---|
|**Straight-On**|Eye-level, orthogonal view|clean product-style composition|
|**3/4 Corner**|Shot from diagonal corner|natural realism, depth, most used|
|**Low 3/4 Corner**|Lower perspective, corner view|intimate, adds presence to frame|
|**High 3/4 Corner**|Slightly elevated view|spatial overview, architectural look|
|**Side Angle**|Off-center horizontal|casual, lifestyle tone|
|**Low Eye-Level**|Slightly under human eye height|moody, dramatic composition|
|**Front-On Slight Tilt**|Gentle off-axis|realism, dynamic imperfection|
|**Oblique Room View**|Deep perspective through space|cinematic, immersive feel|
|**Close-Crop Detail**|Tight frame around subject|texture study, editorial use|
