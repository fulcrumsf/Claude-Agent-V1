### **1. Niche: Retro Nostalgia (80s & 90s Aesthetic)**
🛒 **Why?** Vintage aesthetics are making a comeback, and many Gen Z & Millennials love nostalgia-themed apparel.
👥 **Primary Demographic:**
- **Age:** 18-35
- **Gender:** Unisex
- **Interest:** 80s/90s pop culture, VHS, retro video games, neon colors, Y2K fashion
🎨 **T-Shirt Design Ideas:**
- "Be Kind, Rewind" (with a VHS tape graphic)
- Pixelated "Game Over" text in a neon cyberpunk style
- Retro sun & palm trees with "Summer Vibes 1993"
- A Tamagotchi-style digital pet with "Still Alive… Barely"
- "Made in the 90s" with old-school fonts and graphics

### **2. Niche: Dark Humor & Sarcastic Quotes**
🛒 **Why?** TikTok users love meme culture, sarcasm, and dark humor. These shirts go viral due to relatability.
👥 **Primary Demographic:**
- **Age:** 16-35
- **Gender:** Mostly unisex (skews slightly female)
- **Interest:** Memes, ironic fashion, edgy humor
🎨 **T-Shirt Design Ideas:**
- "I'm Not Arguing, I'm Just Explaining Why I'm Right"
- A loading bar with “Social Battery: 1%”
- "Mentally in My 2000s Emo Phase" (with gothic fonts)
- "I Used to Be Cool. Now I Just Cry A Lot."
- "It's Fine. Everything's Fine." (flames or chaotic doodles)

### **3. Niche: Cottagecore & Nature Aesthetic**
🛒 **Why?** Cottagecore is still trending, and cozy, nature-inspired fashion is a hit with the eco-conscious crowd.
👥 **Primary Demographic:**
- **Age:** 18-30
- **Gender:** Mostly female
- **Interest:** Gardening, slow living, indie fashion, sustainability
🎨 **T-Shirt Design Ideas:**
- "Support Your Local Bees" (with cute bee illustrations)
- A vintage-style mushroom illustration with "Fungi Vibes"
- "Stay Wild, Moon Child" with celestial elements
- "I'd Rather Be in the Woods" (with aesthetic floral line art)
- "Plant More, Worry Less" with watercolor plant designs
