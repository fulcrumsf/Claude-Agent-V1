**Okay if I'm setting up a Beacons, what is the best strategy? Because I know if I have too many links and too many options, it's going to confuse people. So I need to kind of funnel them. And I want to make it a way to where I'm funneling them but I'm also capturing their emails. So how should I do that?**
For maximum affiliate conversions using Beacons (or Linktree-style sites), **simplicity and a clear funnel are critical**. Here’s a high-converting strategy for your niche (AI, animation, automation):
---
**1. Limit Your Main Calls-to-Action (CTAs) to 2–3 Maximum**
- **Prioritize ONE “Hero” Offer:** This could be your highest-commission Skool group, or the best AI animation course/community you recommend.
- **Optionally add:** 1–2 lower-priority CTAs (e.g. a recommended tool, or your personal newsletter).
---
**2. Use a Lead Magnet or Freebie to Capture Emails**
- **Best Method:** At the top, offer a FREE download, cheat sheet, or mini-course (“Free AI Animation Cheat Sheet” or “How to Make Viral Animal Shorts” PDF).
- **Require Email Opt-in:** Use Beacons’ built-in email capture (or integrate with Mailchimp/Airtable). Users must enter email before receiving their download/bonus.
- **Follow Up:** Send email sequence with valuable tips and your affiliate links.
---
**3. Structure Your Beacons Page for Clear Funnel:**
- **Top of Page:** 1–2 sentence statement about the value (“Want to learn viral AI animation fast? Start here…”)
- **First CTA:** “Get Your Free AI Animation Starter Kit” → Email capture → immediate value delivery.
- **Second CTA (after opt-in):** “Join the #1 AI Animation Community (40% Off First Month!)” → Skool affiliate link (or top course/community).
- **Third Optional CTA:** “My Favorite AI Tools for Video Creators” → Link to secondary affiliate tools/courses.
---
**4. Funnel Visualization**
`text[Attention-Grabbing Headline] → [Free Download/Email Opt-In/Lead Magnet] | (capture email here) → [Main Offer Button: Skool Group/Course] | ("Start Your AI Animation Journey") → [Optional: Best AI Tools/Second Community]`
---
**5. Automation**
- Link your email capture to an automated drip sequence (Beacons or your email platform), where you:
    
    - Welcome new subscribers.
    
    - Deliver freebie.
    
    - Share your story/value.
    
    - Push your affiliate offers—do this over several emails, not all at once.
    
---
**6. KEEP IT SIMPLE**
- Avoid featuring more than 3 links on your Beacons page; stick to the _one main offer, one lead magnet, optional secondary tool/course_.
- Make sure every link is visually distinct, with a _clear benefit_ (“Join AI Animation School: 40% OFF” rather than generic “Visit Skool”).
---
## **Summary**
- **Capture emails with a freebie.**
- Funnel all traffic first through email opt-in, THEN to your best affiliate offer.
- Limit total main options (2–3 max).
- Automate email follow-up with affiliate links and community invites.
- Make benefits clear—focus messaging around _viral content, AI learning, and exclusive access_.
This strategy builds your list, maximizes conversions, and keeps your page clean and focused.
**For the lead magnet if I want to do a newsletter, how can you do newsletters for one in Beacons itself? If not can I connect the Beacons emails that I capture to something like Beehiive?**
You have **two strong options** for running a newsletter when capturing emails with Beacons:
---
## 1. **Running Newsletters Directly Within Beacons**
- **Beacons has built-in email marketing:** You can send newsletters, email broadcasts, and automations right from your Beacons dashboard.
- **Features include:** Audience manager, list segmentation, automated campaigns, and broadcast scheduling.
- **Email Limits:** Free plan has 500 sends/month; paid plans allow unlimited sends for a flat rate (around $20/month).
- **Simplicity:** Everything stays in one place — email collection, landing page, and newsletter delivery.
- **How-To:** Go to Audience > Email Broadcasts > Create newsletter, then send or schedule broadcasts to your captured subscribers.
---
## 2. **Connecting Beacons to Beehiiv for Newsletters**
- **Integration Option:** You can connect Beacons email capture to Beehiiv using** Zapier** or by exporting your email list from Beacons and importing into Beehiiv (CSV upload).
- **Automation:** With Zapier, new Beacons subscribers can be sent automatically to your Beehiiv account, so all new signups are in your Beehiiv newsletter.
- **Beehiiv Benefits:** More advanced publisher tools, deliverability, newsletter monetization, heavy creator focus, and integrations with other platforms.
- **How-To:**
    
    1. Use Beacons for email capture with a clear lead magnet.
    
    1. Integrate with Beehiiv via Zapier (direct or multi-step zap), or download your audience as CSV and upload to Beehiiv as needed.
    
    1. Run your newsletter from Beehiiv, and keep using Beacons as your front-end funnel.
    
---
## **Best Practice Summary**
- **For simplicity and speed:** Use Beacons for everything (quickest to set up, fewer moving parts).
- **For advanced newsletter features and growth:** Capture emails with Beacons, then connect to Beehiiv for email marketing and newsletter publishing (especially if you want to grow a large/media brand-style list).
Either way, you can easily **funnel leads** from your Beacons link-in-bio page to your newsletter, collect emails, and send regular value/affiliate offers—whichever tool fits your workflow and growth goals best.
