### ✅ Updated Guardrails & Criteria
**1. Environments**
- Must be _semi- or fully enclosed_ spaces large enough to contain a 25ft animal.
- Think of settings like: domes, massive caves, enclosed glades, large rooms, atriums, greenhouses, ruins with ceilings, underwater air pockets, giant terrariums, ancient chambers, magical bunkers.
**2. Animals**
- Must have defined mouths suitable for stylized, plush-like interpretation.
- Exclude flat-faced, non-mouth-forward species.
- Prefer animals with snouts, muzzles, beaks, or pronounced jawlines.
- Can include extinct or mythical creatures (e.g., sabertooths, dragon-like animals).
**3. Mouth Interiors**
- Must plausibly fit inside a 15x10x25ft space, like themed lounges, playrooms, tiny libraries, cafes, arcades, etc.
- Should match the “immersive plush zone” vibe — cozy, contained, concept-driven spaces.
**4. Lighting**
- Must maintain _clear interior visibility_ like in Image #2.
- High ambient light, glowing textures, cozy warm or cool lighting setups.
