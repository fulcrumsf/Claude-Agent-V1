### 📝 Writing Prompt
  
Write in a slow-burning, emotionally reflective monologue style. Each line should feel intentional—calm, yet piercing. Use short sentences that echo. Speak to the viewer like a trusted friend or inner voice. Blend philosophical insight with poetic melancholy. Minimalist. Understated. No yelling. No excitement. Just raw, focused truth wrapped in stillness.
### ✅ **How to Match the Voice on ElevenLabs or Kokoro TTS**
**🗣 ElevenLabs Voice Matching Tips:**
- Look for voices labeled as **calm**, **philosophical**, or **narrative**
- Suggested models:
    
    - **Adam** – deep and neutral
    
    - **Antoni** or **Joseph** – introspective and slower pacing
    
    - Or train a custom voice using your extracted audio: `philosovibes_audio.wav`
    
**🗣 Kokoro TTS** (if used):
- Prioritize **low pitch**, **slow cadence**, **flat intonation**
- Tweak pause intervals to simulate a meditative delivery
