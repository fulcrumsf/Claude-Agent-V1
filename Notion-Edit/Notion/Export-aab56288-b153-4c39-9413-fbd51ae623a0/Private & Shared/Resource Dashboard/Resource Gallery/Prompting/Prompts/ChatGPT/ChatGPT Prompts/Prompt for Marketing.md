### Pinpoint Target Audience
Describe the perfect group of people who would love [product/service] in [specific niche]. Share lots of details about them, like their age, what they like, what makes them sad or worried, and how they usually act.
### Build a Full Content Marketing Plan
Create a content marketing plan to tell people about my [product/service] in [specific niche] on [specific platform]. Explain how to share it with everyone.
### 7 Day Content Schedule
Create a 7-day content schedule, where I will post 1 piece of content a day that [target audience] would watch and engage with. I post content on [social profiles/channels]
### Come up with a Product or Service to Sell to my Audience
Give me an idea for a product or service I can sell to [Target Audience] that will help them get their [Desired Result]
### Strategy to get Audience to By Product
Create a marketing plan to help sell my [product/service] to [target audience] through [profiles/channels]
### Analyze Competitor's Strategy
Study and look closely at what other companies in our [specific niche] are doing. Then, tell me what they're doing well and what we can do better for our [product/service]
### Craft an Engaging Call to Action
Guide me in crafting compelling calls to action for [product/service] that will drive engagement and conversions.
### Build and Nurture Community
Come up with a plan for making a friendly online community for people who love [product/service] on [platform]. Think of ways to keep them interested and chatting, and set some rules so everyone gets along.
### Implement SEO Techniques
Guide me in implementing the most effective SEO techniques for my [website/blog/social profile about [topic].
### Analyze Performance Metrics
Create a system to monitor and analyze performance metrics for my [website/social profile] in the [specific niche].
