### 🎨 AI Photo Zoom Effect Tutorial (MidJourney + Cling AI)
### 🧰 Tools You’ll Need:
- **MidJourney** (for image generation)
- **Image editor with canvas expansion** (e.g., Photoshop, Canva, or Upscale.media)
- **Video editor** (e.g., Adobe Premiere Pro)
- **Cling AI** (for animation)
---
### ✅ Step-by-Step Instructions:
1. **Generate Your Base Image**
    
    - Go to **MidJourney**
    
    - Enter your desired **text prompt** to generate an AI image
    
1. **Lock in the Style**
    
    - Use **Style Reference** in MidJourney
    
    - Upload your original image so future images match the same style
    
1. **Expand the Image Borders**
    
    - Open your image in an **image editor**
    
    - Expand the **canvas borders** (top, bottom, sides)
    
    - Export the result
    
    - Repeat this process multiple times to create a **sequence of expanded images**
    
1. **Bring Images Into Video Editor**
    
    - Open **Premiere Pro** (or your preferred editor)
    
    - Import the expanded image sequence into your timeline
    
1. **Add Motion with Cling AI**
    
    - Go to **Cling AI**
    
    - Upload one of the images
    
    - Use a prompt like **"Camera Slow Zoom In"** to animate it
    
    - Download the animation and add it to your video timeline
    
1. **Repeat for Zoom Out or Additional Effects**
    
    - Repeat the Cling AI process with different prompts if needed (e.g., "Zoom Out")
