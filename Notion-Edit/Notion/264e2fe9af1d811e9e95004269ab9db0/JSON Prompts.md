> [!important]
> 
> JSON Prompting Defined:  
>   
> Below is an example of a JSON Prompt. Get can have Chatgpt build you one based on the attributes you set. This helps keep your AI image generation consistent when trying to achieve a similar look or subject type over and over again.

> [!important]
> 
> EXAMPLE: Just change the parts that are highlighted to generate a different image but keeping the structure of the prompt the same.
> 
> {  
> "subject": "==cyberpunk samurai==",  
> "setting": "==rainy neon city at night==",  
> "style": "==anime==",  
> "action": "==drawing katana==",  
> "camera": "==low angle==",  
> "lighting": "==neon reflections==",  
> "mood": "==tense=="  
> }
