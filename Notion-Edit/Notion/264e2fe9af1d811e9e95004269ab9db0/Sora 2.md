### Sora 2 Prompt Template
---
## **🎬**
## **SORA 2 PROMPT TEMPLATE BLOCK**
```
# 🎥 SORA 2 VIDEO PROMPT DRAFT
## 🎯 CONCEPT
(Briefly describe the core idea, subject, or theme.
Example: “A lonely robot finds friendship with a lost child in a rainy city.”)
---
## 🎞 FORMAT
- [ ] Shortform (Vertical) – TikTok / Shorts
- [ ] Longform (Horizontal) – YouTube / Film
---
## 🧠 TONE & STYLE
(Choose or describe)
- Emotional / Heartfelt
- Suspense / Mystery
- Humor / Relatable
- Awe / Cinematic
- Documentary / Realistic
- UGC / Handheld
---
## 🎬 STRUCTURE
### **[Visual Hook]**
Describe the *very first frame* — the image that instantly captures attention.
It should work **without text or dialogue**, immediately readable and emotionally engaging.
*(e.g., “An elderly woman stands trembling on a tall ladder cleaning a chandelier.”)*
### **[Build-Up / Context]**
Set up anticipation, curiosity, or emotional depth.
Keep it visually understandable and consistent in tone.
*(e.g., “She stretches higher, the ladder wobbles — a dog barks below.”)*
### **[Payoff / Resolution]**
Deliver the emotional or narrative moment that satisfies or surprises the viewer.
*(e.g., “The ladder tips — the chandelier’s chain catches her, saving her. She laughs in relief.”)*
---
## 🎥 CINEMATIC DETAILS (Use only if they add value)
```
[Camera Type] e.g., Alexa Mini LF / iPhone 15 Pro / Drone FPV
[Camera Angle] e.g., Low-angle close-up / Overhead wide / POV shot
[Lighting] e.g., Golden hour / Harsh morning sun / Neon reflections
[Atmosphere] e.g., Fog / Light rain / Dust motes in air
[Sound FX] e.g., Soft footsteps, faint heartbeat, door creak
[Environment] e.g., Urban rooftop at dusk / Mountain cabin interior
```
---
## 🧩 SEQUENCE (Beat-based pacing)
Use natural beats, not timestamps. Each beat = one coherent shot.
```
Scene 1 – Opening visual hook.
Scene 2 – Emotional or narrative setup.
Scene 3 – Rising anticipation or action shift.
Scene 4 – Emotional or visual payoff.
```
---
## 💡 OPTIONAL: LONGFORM STRUCTURE (for narrative pacing)
If longform, use this rollercoaster flow:
```
[Hook + VO Hook]
[Context]
[Small Twist]
[Further Context]
[Plot Twist]
[Closing Beat / Cliffhanger]
```
---
## 🔁 VARIATIONS
Use this section to create multiple tone/style passes:
- Variation 1: Cinematic realism
- Variation 2: Emotional / tender
- Variation 3: Comedic or UGC-style
---
## 🧱 FINAL OUTPUT EXAMPLE
**Prompt Example:**
```
[Camera Type] Handheld smartphone
[Lighting] Overcast, soft daylight
[Scene 1] – A drenched kitten meows from under a park bench.
[Scene 2] – A boy with a bright yellow raincoat kneels and opens his umbrella.
[Scene 3] – The kitten hesitates, then climbs onto his lap.
[Payoff] – The two walk off under the same umbrella through the rain.
Tone: Gentle, heartwarming, cinematic simplicity.
```
---
### ✅ TIPS
- Use the **Sora2 Official Prompt Guide** as your rulebook for format, duration, and API parameter awareness.
- Every scene should be **emotionally legible** and visually consistent.
- Think **storyboard**, not script.
- Avoid dialogue unless it drives emotion or clarity.
- Keep descriptions visual, simple, and sensory.
```
