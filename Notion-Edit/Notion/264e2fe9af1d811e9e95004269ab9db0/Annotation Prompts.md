### 🔍 What Is Visual Prompting?
**Visual Prompting** is the practice of using an **existing image** as a guide or instruction to help the AI **understand what should happen** in a new generated image. Instead of (or in addition to) text prompts, you use **visual annotations**—arrows, boxes, labels—to **show what objects are doing, where they are, or how they move**.
