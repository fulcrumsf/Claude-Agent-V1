## 🔒 CHARACTER CONSISTENCY (ID LOCK)
- **\#Shiba01** — Shiba Inu, reddish-brown fur, cream underbelly, curled tail, upright ears, wearing a **small black Japanese randoseru school backpack** strapped high on top of its back (between shoulders). Alert, determined, but wholesome expression.
- **\#Grandpa01** — Elderly Japanese man, wiry frame, slightly hunched posture. **Traditional outfit**: light indigo _samue_ (work jacket + loose pants), white undershirt, and worn straw sandals. **Details**: wrinkled face, sparse gray hair, thin mustache, gentle but stubborn expression.
- **\#Bike01** — Pastel Japanese _mamachari_ bicycle with a chrome front basket, tassels on handlebars, lightly smoking **rear wheel**.
- **\#Balloons01** — Exactly **6 helium balloons**, always the same **colors** (red, yellow, green, blue, purple, orange), tied securely to the **rear of #Bike01** (behind seat), floating consistently above.
- **\#Street01** — Quiet **suburban Kyoto street**, narrow asphalt road with cracks, low tiled-roof houses, wooden fences, small gardens with potted plants, telephone poles with wires, and cherry blossom trees.
## 📖 SYNOPSIS (Refined)
In a quiet Kyoto suburb, a shiba inu with a tiny black school backpack notices an elderly grandpa wobbling along on his pastel mamachari bicycle. Six bright balloons bob behind the bike as its rear wheel begins to smoke and spark. The shiba barks and chases after him, pulling the balloons away just before the bike bursts into flames. Safe on the street corner, the grandpa sighs in relief and pats the shiba’s head — a raw, heartwarming “caught on camera” rescue moment.
