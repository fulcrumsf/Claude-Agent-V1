# Channels & Descriptions
The below three channels give us the option to play around with different styles. They are broad enough we can try out different sub-niches and if we see enough traction we can spin-off those into their own channels. The idea is to start building tailored automations based on the trends and analytics to create and autopost to those channels. The more hands-off we can be the more we are able to chase the passion project that will require more of a hands on approach.
  
### Important Please Read

> [!important]
> 
> All content created for any **Uno Mas Creative LLC** channel listed in the Agreement must be **published on the corresponding Uno Mas Creative LLC channels first** before being shared or reposted anywhere else. This includes any personal or third-party accounts operated by team members or contributors.
> 
>   
> 
> This requirement ensures that **Uno Mas Creative LLC** remains the original publisher, preserving the content’s monetization eligibility under [YouTube’s reused content policy](https://support.google.com/youtube/community-guide/271248162/%F0%9F%94%8E-faq-reused-content-youtube%E2%80%99s-partner-program?hl=en). Posting the content elsewhere before it appears on the designated Uno Mas Creative LLC channels may cause it to be flagged as reused and therefore ineligible for monetization.  
>   
> If content is uploaded or shared elsewhere before it appears on an official Uno Mas Creative LLC channels, YouTube may classify the Company’s upload as **“reused content”**, which can **jeopardize monetization eligibility for the entire channel**, not just the individual video. This policy helps ensure Uno Mas Creative remains the original publisher of all content produced under this Agreement, protecting the monetization status of every associated channel.
