### Story Idea Funnel Prompt
## 🧠 STORY IDEA GENERATION TEMPLATE (FOR CHATGPT)
## 🔧 How It Works:
Whenever you see this symbol: **🟨 [INSERT HERE]**
→ Replace it with your own topic, event, or idea.
It can be vague or specific — just something you’re curious about or find strange, powerful, or emotional.
## 🟢 Step 1 — Ask for Unusual Reason

> Hey, I’ve been thinking about this lately: 🟨 [INSERT TOPIC].
> 
> _Can you give me 10 unusual, surprising, or underexplored reasons why this happened / existed / became important?_
> 
> _Please number them clearly. I’m looking for story angles that most people don’t talk about._
## 🟢 Step 2 — Zoom Into One Reason

> I think reason number 🟨 [INSERT NUMBER] is really interesting.
> 
> _Can you break down that one reason into 5 short, specific ideas — events, turning points, contradictions, or facts — that are shocking, emotional, or visually powerful?_
> 
> _Keep them tightly related to that reason._

### Scripts Prompt Template
## Input the topic information for the hook you need help from CHATGPT and copy and paste it in CHATGPT 🤖
Write a dramatic short story in this **exact format and pacing** for the following topic _**🟨 [INSERT TOPIC]**_Each sentence should be short and cinematic — like subtitles. No long paragraphs.
Follow this 7-part structure **exactly**:

> 🟢 **CONTEXT (PART 1)**
> 
> - Start with the date and place: “It’s [year]. [City or country].”
> 
> - Introduce characters and setup in simple, factual lines
> 
> - Add a cultural or shocking historical norm (“Even marry — because that’s how royalty worked back then.”)
> 
> 🔸 **SMALL TWIST (PART 2)**
> 
> - Use a transitional line like “And for a while… it worked.”
> 
> - Add a sentence or two showing early success or tension building
> 
> ⚫ **PLOT TWIST (PART 3)**
> 
> - Show what went wrong
> 
> - Add betrayal, ambition, or power struggle
> 
> - End with a dramatic shift (exile, downfall, turning point)
> 
> 🟢 **CONTEXT (PART 4)**
> 
> - Show how the main character responded
> 
> - Use short action sentences (e.g. “She camped outside the walls. Built an army.”)
> 
> - Mention an important alliance if relevant
> 
> 🔸 **SMALL TWIST (PART 5)**
> 
> - Use a quiet tension line (e.g. “And one night… she snuck back in.”)
> 
> - Do **not** overexplain — it’s a stealth or setup move
> 
> ⚫ **FINAL CONSEQUENCE (PART 6)**
> 
> - Reveal the major event or fallout
> 
> - Keep it mysterious (“No one knows how.” “But one thing was clear…”)
> 
> 🟡 **REVEAL (PART 7)**
> 
> - Final punchline with identity:
>     
>     “And the [girl/man/place] who did it… was [name].”
>     
> 
> ---
> 
> Tone should be visual, cold, and factual — like a narrated historical scene.
> 
> No internal thoughts. No explanations. Just actions and outcomes.

> When I give you a topic, follow this structure exactly.
### Hooks Prompt Template
## Input the topic information for the hook you need help from CHATGPT and copy and paste it in CHATGPT 🤖
I want you to help me create a short, dramatic hook in this format for _**🟨 [INSERT TOPIC]**_Don’t mention names, countries, or places directly. Use words like _this man_, _this woman_, _this city_, _this village_, etc.
Here’s the structure I want:
- Start with:
    
    "**This [character type]**"
    
    _(e.g. “This girl,” “This soldier,” “This preacher,” “This town,” etc.)_
    
- Add a short phrase describing where or when, in parentheses:
    
    _(e.g. “(in a war-torn village)”, “(in the 1980s)”, “(deep in the jungle)”)_
    
- Then, add one **powerful trait** or **unique detail** that makes them special
    
    _(e.g. “taught herself to fight”, “never spoke a word”, “wore a stolen uniform”)_
    
- Then, describe **one key action** they did
    
    _(e.g. “led a rebellion”, “built a secret bunker”, “married a king”)_
    
- Then, pause (add a line break or em dash)
- Finally, give a **twist or consequence**. A reversal. Something unexpected, ironic, tragic, or mysterious.
    
    _(e.g. “— and was executed by the people she saved.”, “But no one knows why she disappeared the next day.”, “And that’s how she started a war.”)_
    
Example:

> This girl (in medieval France) dressed like a soldier, claimed she spoke to God, and led an army — just to be betrayed by the king she fought for.
### Example of Good Script
## Example of the script and hook we built:
**LEGEND** :
🟢 **CONTEXT**
🔸 **SMALL TWIST**
⚫ **PLOT TWIST**
🟡 **REVEAL**
_HOOK : THIS 16 YEAR OLD GIRL SAVED FRANCE DRESSED AS A BOY_

> 🟢 It’s 1429.

> 🟢 France is collapsing — cities fall, the king hides, the war nearly lost.

> 🟢 In a quiet village, a teenage girl says she hears voices.

> 🔸 Holy voices, telling her to save France. She’s sixteen. A peasant. Illiterate.

> 🟢 She cuts her hair, puts on armor, rides to court.
> 
> 🔸 They laugh. Some think she’s a boy.

> 🔸 But she convinces the king. They give her an army.

> 🟢 And she wins. She breaks the siege.

> 🟢 Crowns the prince. France calls her a hero.

> 🟢 For a moment — she feels untouchable.
> 
> **⚫ Until the king betrays her.**
> 
> 🟢 Refuses to protect her. Lets her fall.

> 🟢 She’s captured. Put on trial. Called a witch.
> 
> 🔸 **Burned at nineteen.**

> 🟢 Years later, they call her a saint. And the girl who saved France…

> 🟡 was Joan of Arc.
