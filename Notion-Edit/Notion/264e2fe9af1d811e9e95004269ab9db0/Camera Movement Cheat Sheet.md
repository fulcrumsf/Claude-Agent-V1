### **📌 HOW TO USE:**
1. **Go to**: `/create` in MidJourney (if using the experimental Video tool)
1. Click on **“Low Motion” > Manual**
1. In the **“Motion Prompt”** box, type the movement (example: `slow pan left`)
1. Combine with visual style in your main prompt.
1. **Still Camera**
    
    _(No movement. Scene remains static like a painting.)_
    
    ✅ Use: `still camera` or leave motion box blank for ultra-minimal movement.
    
1. **Slow Pan Left / Right**
    
    _(Horizontal camera move — slowly reveals scene from one side to the other.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `slow pan left` or `slow pan right`
    
1. **Push-In**
    
    _(Camera slowly moves toward the subject — creates emotional focus.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `slow push-in` or `gradual zoom-in`
    
1. **Pull-Out**
    
    _(Camera slowly moves backward — reveals context or isolation.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `slow pull-out` or `zoom out from subject`
    
1. **Tilt Up / Down**
    
    _(Vertical camera move — reveals sky, mountains, or below.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `slow tilt up` or `tilt down from clouds to ground`
    
1. **Tracking Shot (Side)**
    
    _(Camera moves with the subject sideways — feels like following.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `tracking shot side view` or `sideways tracking motion`
    
1. **Orbit / Circular Movement**
    
    _(Camera moves around the subject — gives 3D depth to a still pose.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `orbit camera movement`, `circular dolly`
    
1. **Handheld Camera Vibe**
    
    _(Slight shake, imperfect stability — adds realism and grit.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `handheld camera motion` (⚠️ mid-level movement only)
    
1. **Overhead Drone Shot**
    
    _(Bird’s-eye view, floating top-down camera — great for scenery.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `aerial shot moving forward`, `drone view`
    
1. **Steadicam Walk-Through**
    
    _(Gliding forward motion, like walking through a hallway.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `walking forward`, `steadicam corridor walkthrough`
    
---
1. **Parallax Slide**
    
    _(Foreground and background move at different speeds — feels layered.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `parallax motion`, `foreground slide left`
    
1. **Zolly (Dolly Zoom Effect)**
    
    _(Zoom in while pulling back — creates visual warping.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `dolly zoom`, `vertigo effect`
    
    ⚠️ Experimental in MidJourney, may glitch.
    
1. **Jittery Jumpcut**
    
    _(Quick movement between slight angles — great for edgy or chaotic scenes.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `jumpcut movement`, `jittery fast shake`
    
1. **Crane Shot Down**
    
    _(Camera descends slowly from above — epic reveal.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `crane down`, `descending shot`
    
1. **Dolly Through Object**
    
    _(Camera moves through a doorway, smoke, or portal.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `dolly through`, `move through fog`
    
1. **360º Spin**
    
    _(Full horizontal rotation around a point.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `360 spin around`, `circular pan`
    
1. **Whip Pan**
    
    _(Fast blur pan left or right — creates action.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `whip pan right`, `quick pan motion`
    
1. **First-Person Gliding**
    
    _(Like a drone or FPV camera through space.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `first-person glide`, `flying POV`
    
1. **Zoom-Pulse**
    
    _(Zoom in and out subtly — creates breathing visual effect.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `pulse zoom`, `zoom drift`
    
1. **Time-Lapse Drift**
    
    _(Simulates a time-lapse where the camera moves slowly.)_
    
    ✅ Prompt: `time-lapse motion`, `slow drift through time`
    
---
### 🔧 BONUS TIPS:
- Add speed modifiers like: `fast`, `slow`, `gradual`, `subtle`, `jittery`
- Combine: `slow pan left with push-in`, `drone overhead with orbit motion`
- Mix with **mood**: `slow tilt up — melancholic`, `tracking shot — dreamy`
