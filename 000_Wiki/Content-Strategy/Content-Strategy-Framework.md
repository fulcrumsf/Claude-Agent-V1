# Content Strategy Framework — Summary

Source: [[../../001_Architecture/Skills/Content-Strategy-Framework.md]]

Strategic planning framework for content calendars, audience segmentation, and revenue attribution across all brands and platforms.

**Core principles:** audience-first, revenue integration on every piece, franchisability, cross-platform from inception, data-driven (not guess-driven).

**Structure:**
1. **Audience Definition** — primary persona (demographics + psychographics + pain points + desires + trust triggers), behavior patterns (peak times, format preference, monetization receptiveness), and competitive landscape (top-3 competitors + market gaps).
2. **Content Pillars** — 3–5 core themes per brand, each tagged with target segment, platform fit, content type, cinematic style, and monetization pathway.
3. **Content Calendar Strategy** — sequencing pillars across the calendar, with weekly cadence by brand.
4. **Production Pipeline Integration** — every calendar slot maps to the cinematic style framework and the video production workflow.
5. **Revenue Attribution** — direct, affiliate, and funnel paths logged per piece so winners can be doubled down.

Used by every brand (Anomalous-Wild, Board-Nomad, Unamoss-Creative, Ikigai) when building or revising their content systems.
