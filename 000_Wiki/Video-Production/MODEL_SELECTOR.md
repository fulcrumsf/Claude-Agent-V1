## Summary

A decision-framework guide for selecting the correct AI generation model per cinematic style, output type, and quality tier. Centred on kie.ai pricing (significantly cheaper than fal.ai) and includes a quick-reference table mapping each of the six cinematic styles to a recommended model.

For static imagery (Archival B&W, 3D Synthetic, Satellite/Map, Wildlife stills, Infographic) the default is Nano Banana 2 at $0.04-0.09 per asset. For video, Kling 2.1 Pro handles HD Underwater B-roll at $0.125-0.25 per 5-second clip; Veo 3 Quality is reserved for hero shots and cinematic Wildlife video at $2.00 per 8-second clip.

The decision flow walks through three steps: output type (image vs. video), quality requirement (rough cut, broadcast, hero), and budget tier. This is the operational lookup used during scene-by-scene production to keep cost predictable while matching each shot to the right model capability.
