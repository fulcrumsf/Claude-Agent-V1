# Cinematic Styles — Summary

Source: [[../../001_Architecture/Skills/Cinematic-Styles.md]]

Six production methodologies covering most video output:

1. **Archival B&W Documentary** — high contrast monochrome for historical/investigative content. Primary: kie.ai Veo (40% cheaper than fal.ai).
2. **3D Synthetic** — clean rendered geometry for tech/SaaS explainers. Primary: fal.ai Kling Synthetic; higher quality but 50% costlier.
3. **Satellite/Map** — overhead/aerial perspectives for travel and journey storytelling. Primary: kie.ai Veo (35% cheaper).
4. **HD Underwater** — crystal-clear marine footage with volumetric blue-green lighting. Primary: kie.ai (45% cheaper). Channel default for Anomalous-Wild.
5. **Wildlife** — natural cinematic photography with shallow DOF and golden-hour color temps. Primary: kie.ai (50% cheaper, preferred for series).
6. **Scientific Infographic** — animated data and diagrams for STEM/educational explainers.

**Cost rule:** kie.ai is the default — 30–70% cheaper than fal.ai. Escalate to fal.ai only when ultra-high resolution or specific Kling features are required. Each style has parameter presets (grain, DOF, color temp, particle density) documented in the source file.
