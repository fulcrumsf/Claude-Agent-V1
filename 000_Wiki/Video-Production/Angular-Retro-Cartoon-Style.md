# Angular Retro Cartoon Style

## What It Is
An image generation prompt for Niji 6 that produces a distinct retro-futuristic sci-fi look with blocky anatomy and clean cel shading.

## Key Concepts
- Relies on Niji 6 capabilities with a 16:9 aspect ratio
- Utilizes "angular retro cartoon style" and "blocky anatomy" keywords
- Achieves sharp aesthetics through "clean cel shading"
- Thematic focus on "retro-futuristic tech"
