## Ecosystem Map

Content (video + social) → audience growth → affiliate clicks + store traffic + digital product sales → email list → repeat revenue.

The video engine is the top of the funnel. Every department feeds the others.

## How to Work With Tony

- **Systems thinker** — wants things automated eventually; always flag automation opportunities
- **Trial/manual phase first** — quality before scale across most departments
- **Final decision maker** — confirms all publishing and spending before they go live
- **Voice input** — prompts are casual and flowing; expect to pull structure from unstructured thinking
- **Brainstorming mode** — when Tony says this, think and talk only; do not build until told
